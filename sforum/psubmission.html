Paper Submission


We encourage undergraduate students to submit an article on their research projects, including technical and/or scientific. Submissions from beginner graduate students, starting or willing to start their master project, will also be considered.

The students are invited to submit papers having 2 to 4 pages, in English, in two-column format following the ACM Press guidelines. The best papers will be invited for oral presentations.

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: Extended - July 02sd, 2012
  • Notification of Acceptance: July 23th , 2012
  • Camera-Ready Deadline : August 2nd, 2012
